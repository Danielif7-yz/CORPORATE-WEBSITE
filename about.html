About Us

Incorperated in 2003 AB United Services Limited is a high profiled Engineering and Environmental Services Company in Nigeria. We are one of the leading indigenous Engineering and Environmental Services Company in the Niger Delta Region of Nigeria.


ABUNITED SERVICES LIMITED is an Indigenous Engineering And Environmental Services Company with over 17 years of experience in both the oil and gas and the non- oil and gas sector of the Nigerian Industries. The Company was incorporated in Nigeria in 2003 with the Registration No: RC: 479114 as a limited liability company. Since in business, ABUS has successfully carried out services in its field of operations (onshore and offshore pipelines/storage facility maintenance services, corrosion control and protection engineering– sand/grit blasting and painting /coating of storage tanks and vessels etc. Plant Maintenance, Rehabilitations, Nondestructive testing, Hydro testing, Mechanical rotating, Welding and Fabrication, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil Engineering and Environmental services) to both producing and the non-producing companies in the oil and gas sectors and the non oil and gas sector.


ABUS is equipped with seasoned &knowledge based highly skilled Nigerian with wealth of experience and technical capabilities to serve the oil and gas and the non-oil and gas sector. We provide the most reliable, transparent and quality services tailored to costumer’s specifications and expectation.
