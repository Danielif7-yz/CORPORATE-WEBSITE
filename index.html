    Who We Are

    Incorperated in 2003 AB United Services Limited is a high profiled Engineering and Environmental Services Company in Nigeria. We are one of the leading indigenous Engineering and Environmental Services Company in the Niger Delta Region of Nigeria.

    We are registered Contractors/Consultants to Nigerian National Petroleum Corperation(NNPC) and all its subsidiaries:- Port Harcourt Refinery (PHRC), Kaduna Refinery and Petrochemical Company (KRPC), Warri Refinery Company, The National Petroleum Investment Management Services (NAPIMS), National Engineering & Technical Company Limited (NETCO),Nigeria Pipelines And Storage Company (NPSC), Nigerian Gas Company (NGC), HYSON Nigeria Limited; Nigeria Liquified Natural Gas(NLNG) Nigeria Agip Oil Company (NAOC), Niger Delta Development Commission (NDDC), Alpha Integrated and Energy Services, GTH Porthacourt, LMH Porthacourt etc and have executed jobs successfuly with efficiency.
