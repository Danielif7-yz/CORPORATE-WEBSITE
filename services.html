AB UNITED SERVICES LTD

AB United Serivces Limited is one of the leading indigenous engineering and environmental services company in the Niger Delta Region of Nigeria. Since in business, ABUS has sucscessfully carried out services in its field of operations in onshore and offshore pipelines/storage facility maintenance services (corrosion control and protection engineering– sand/grit blasting and painting /coating of storage tanks and vessels etc), Plant Maintenance, Rehabilitations, Nondestructive testing, Hydro testing, Mechanical rotating, Welding and Fabrication, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil Engineering and Environmental services, to both producing and the non-producing companies in the oil and gas sectors and the non oil and gas sector.
